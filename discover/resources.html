Intermediaries Research

Report
2023 Dr. Tegan Bristow

This report investigates the role of intermediaries within Africa's digital creative economy, examining how organisations and individuals act as connectors between artists, technologists, funders, and audiences. Drawing on interviews and case studies from across the continent, it explores questions of trust, value exchange, and the structural conditions that shape creative ecosystems in rapidly evolving digital landscapes.

Celebrating 10 Years of African Digital Creativity

Report
2023 Eduardo Cachucho

A comprehensive overview of the 10th edition of the Fak'ugesi African Digital Innovation Festival, highlighting its role in advancing Africa's digital creative industries through innovation, collaboration, and cultural storytelling. The report documents five days of activities that included industry conferences, expert panels, student programmes, family workshops, exhibitions, hackathons, masterclasses, networking events, live music performances, and the Fak'ugesi Awards.
