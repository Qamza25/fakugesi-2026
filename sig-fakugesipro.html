An evolution of the Intermediaries legacy programme – Fak'ugesiPRO is the Festival's dedicated talks and industry platform. It is a working environment curated for organisations and practitioners operating at the intersection of creativity, technology, research, and policy, and for the professionals who move between those worlds with intent.

What is Fak'ugesiPRO ?

Studios, cultural institutions, production houses, government agencies, development finance organisations, tertiary institutions, and technology companies gather to address the practical realities shaping Africa's digital ecosystems. Sessions span intellectual property, investment access, platform regulation, creative infrastructure, skills pipelines, cross-border collaboration amongst others. Delegates gain access to facilitated introductions, closed roundtables, and strategic conversations linked to the broader Festival community and networks.

Fak'ugesiPRO is not a conference. It is where organisations that have decided to engage seriously with African digital creative infrastructure come to do the work of engagement: identifying partners, structuring agreements, and building lasting relationships.

In Conversation

ACIII Booklet Launch

The flagship session formally launched the Afrique Créative III Advocacy Booklet, presenting key insights on investment gaps, financing needs, and sustainability pathways in Africa's Cultural and Creative Industries (CCIs).

Panel Discussion

A Seat at the Table of Change

Visionary minds from sustainable food systems, digital innovation, fashion, and cultural storytelling examined how Africa can grow, share, and sustain life.

Keynote Provocation

Electric Futures

Multidisciplinary artist Xabiso Vili explored African Futurism as a radical act of creative agency. Through XR, avatars, gaming, and speculative aesthetics, the session positioned African creators as architects of new systems.

Workshop

The AI Workshop

This hands-on session invited participants to dive into the world of generative AI and discover tools that enhance creative projects from brainstorming to final production.
