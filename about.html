Who We Are

Since 2014, Fak’ugesi African Digital & Innovation Festival has brought together artists, technologists, researchers, designers, entrepreneurs and cultural practitioners to exchange ideas, build collaborations and share new work.

Founded through the Wits School of Arts by Prof Christo Doherty, whose pioneering work in digital arts education and interdisciplinary innovation helped establish the foundations for South Africa’s digital creative sector, the festival found its home at the Tshimologong Digital & Innovation Precinct.


Today, Fak’ugesi is one of the continent’s leading platforms for African digital creativity, bringing together practitioners from across Africa and beyond to imagine, build and share new digital futures.
