Pitchathon

Building Tomorrow
One Pitch at a Time

The stage where ideas and prototypes stop existing as slides and start facing the conditions of the real world. Participants present projects to a panel of investors, venture capitalists, accelerator leads, producers, and industry strategists working across African markets and cultural sectors. Potential projects range from animation, gaming and immersive technology to AI in music and various other fields.

The process is rigorous and always specific. Participants are challenged on sustainability, audience understanding, intellectual property, market positioning, and operational clarity. Strong projects stand to receive mentorship, partnership, and/or funding support.

Applications are open to digital practitioners, collectives, and early-stage ventures from across the continent. Ventures are evaluated on conceptual rigour, market readiness, and their contribution to the wider African Imaginaries ecosystem.
