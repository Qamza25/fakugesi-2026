African Games. Global Play.

African-made and global games take centre stage at this dedicated space. From independent developers to established studios, or new voices developed through the Digital Lab Africa pipeline and our Gaming Hub, Dala Khona is where game development becomes visible, playable, and public. Discover new games. Meet the developers. Test prototypes. Compete. Come back. Players, families, students, educators, researchers, industry professionals, and people who have never held a controller before are all welcomed.
