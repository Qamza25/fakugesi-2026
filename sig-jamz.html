A Frequency of Gathering

The festival's high-pressure, rapid-prototyping environment is a space where makers stop talking about ideas and start building them. Structured around tight time constraints and open provocations, Jamz brings together practitioners from animation, game development, design, and digital art to collaborate under real creative pressure.
Jamz is open to practitioners at all career stages. The entry criterion is genuine commitment to the making process and willingness to collaborate under pressure. Provocations are drawn from the Festival Frequencies: Memory Engines, Imagination Engines, and Future Engines.

2026 Animation
Challenge

A multi-round elimination animation tournament

Tournament of Power

This year's Animation Jam, presented by Mollo Animation Academy and Studio, adopts a structured heats format that mirrors the pace and pressure of professional production environments. Teams will compete through successive rounds of creative challenges, responding to new briefs, technical constraints and limited production time. At the end of each round, selected teams will advance, culminating in a final showcase where the strongest concepts, execution and collaboration will determine the overall winner.

2026 Gaming
Challenge

Competitive Development League

For the Love of Making Games

This challenge brings together developers, designers, artists, writers and audio creators to build original playable games within a live competition format. Working in multidisciplinary teams, participants respond to themed briefs, develop prototypes under strict time constraints and present their work to a panel of industry judges. The challenge rewards creativity, technical execution, collaboration and the ability to transform ambitious ideas into compelling playable experiences.
